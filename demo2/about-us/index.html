Who We Are

Vikens Trucks is a subsidiary of Vikens Group of Companies. Vikens Trucks is a Uganda based trucking company that is aimed at handling transportation of different merchandise for large, medium and small companies in Uganda. Vikens is a third-party logistics company basically focusing on the general movement of merchandise from one point to another and has chosen the trucking industry since the growth prospects are encouraging and stable, with trucking dominating the freight industry in this country.

What We Do

Vikens Trucks is a company that was established in 2017 to deliver excellent services in terms of helping our customers move their goods, equipment, materials and personal items from one destination to another at an affordable rate while providing you with corporate services. We operate under the Vikens & Anderson group of companies

Who We Are

With more than 3 years of experience in the logistics industry, Vikens trucks is one of the leading Logistics company's in Uganda.

Vikens is a third-party logistics company basically focusing on the general movement of merchandise from one point to another and has chosen the trucking industry since the growth prospects are encouraging and stable, with trucking dominating the freight industry in this country.
